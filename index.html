PERIODIC TABLE:-

                  PERIODIC TABLE IS A TABLE WHICH CONTAINS ELEMENTS IN IT IN AN ODERED WAY.

ELEMENTS:-

                   ELEMENTS ARE SUBSTANS WHICH ARE PURE. ELEMENTS ARE DIVIDED INTO 4 BLOCKS BASED ON THEIR PROPERTY. S-BLOCK, P-BLOCK, D-BLOCK, F-BLOCK

S-BLOCK ELEMENTS:-

                           S-BLOCK ELEMENTS contain 2 groups in olden periodic table they are named by IA group elements and IIA group elements. In modern periodic table they are named by 1th group and 2nd group ELEMENTS

 

 

 

IA GROUP or 1st GROUP ELEMENTS:-

IA GROUP or 1ST GROUP ELEMENTS are called ALKALI METALS.

 

HYDROGEN, LITHIUM, SODIUM, POTASIUM, RUBEDIUM, CAESIUM, FRANCIUM.

IIA GROUP or 2ND GROUP ELEMENTS:-

IIA GROUP or 2ND GROUP ELEMENTS are also called ALKALINE EARTH METALS.

 BERYLIUM, MAGNESIUM, CALCIUM, STRONTIUM, BARIUM, RADIUM.

 

P-BLOCK ELEMENTS:-

P-BLOCK ELEMENTS contain 6 groups: IIIA GROUP or 13TH GROUP ELEMENTS, IVA GROUP or 14TH GROUP ELEMENTS, VA GROUP or 15TH GROUP ELEMENTS, VIA GROUP or 16TH GROUP ELEMENTS, VIIA GROUP or 17TH GROUP ELEMENTS, VIIIA GROUP or 18TH GROUP ELEMENTS.

 

IIIA GROUP or 13TH GROUP ELEMENTS:-

IIIA GROUP or 13TH GROUP ELEMENTS are also called BORON FAMILY.

BORON, ALUMINIUM, GALLIUM, INDIUM, THALLIUM, NEHORIUM.

 

 

 

 

 

 

IVA GROUP or 14TH GROUP ELEMENTS:-

IVA GROUP or 14TH GROUP ELEMENTS are also called as CARBON FAMILY.

CARBON, SILCON, GERMENIUM, TIN, LEAD, FLEROVIUM.

VA GROUP or 15TH GROUP ELEMENTS:-

VA GROUP or 15TH GROUP ELEMENTS are also called as NITROGEN FAMILY / PNICTOGENS.

NITROGEN, PHOSPHUROUS, ARSENIC, ANTIMONY, BISMUTH, MASCOVIUM.

VIA GROUP or 16TH GROUP ELEMENTS:-

VIA GROUP ELEMENTS or 16TH GROUP ELEMENTS are also called as OXYGEN FAMILY / CHALCOGENS.

OXYGEN, SULPHOR, SELINUM, TELLURIUM, POLONIUM, LIVERANORIUM.

 

VIIA GROUP or 17TH ELEMENTS:-

VIIA GROUP or 17TH GROUP ELEMENTS are also called as HALOGENS.

FLUORINE, CHLORINE, BROMINE, IODINE, ASTATINE, TENNISEN.

 

VIIIA GROUP or 18TH GROUP ELEMENTS:-

VIIIA GROUP or 18TH GROUP ELEMENTS are also called as NOBLE GASSES / O GROUP ELEMENTS / INERT GASSES / RARE GASSES.

HELIUM, NEON, ARGON, KRYPTON, XENON, REDON, OGANISSAN.

 

D-BLOCK ELEMENTS:-

D-BLOCK ELEMENTS contain 4 series: 3D SERIES, 4D SERIES, 5D SERIES and 6D SERIES.

3D SERIES:-

SCANDIUM, TITANIUM, VENADIUM, CHROMIUM, MAGNESE, IRON, COBALT, NICKLE, COPPER, ZINC.

4D SERIES:-

YITRIUM, ZIRCONIUM, NIOBIUM, MOLBDENUM, TECHNETIUM, RUTHENIUM, RHODIUM, PALLADIUM, SILVER, CADMIUM.

5D SERIES:-

LUTETIUM, HAFNIUM, TANTALUM, TUNGSTEN, RHENIUM, OSMIUM, IRIDIUM, PLATINUM, AURUM, MERCURY.

6D SERIES:-

LAWRENSIUM, RUTHERFORDIUM, DUBNIUM, SEABORGIUM, BHORIUM, HASSIUM, MEITNERIUM, DARMSTADIUM, ROENTGENIUM, COPPERNICIUM.

F-BLOCK ELEMENTS:-

The space between BARIUM to LUTHENTIUM and RADIUM to LAWRENSIUM is indicated with 1star and 2stars that is exact location of F-BLOCK ELEMENTS. It is divided into2 series 4F SERIES and 5F SERIES.

4F SERIES:-

The space between BARIUM and LUTENTIUM is indicated with a star that star indicates 4F SERIES. LANTHANUM, CERIUM, PRASEODYNIUM, NEODYNIUM, PROETHIUM, SAMARIUM, EUROPEUM, GADOLINIUM, TERBIUM, DYSPROSIUM, HOLOMIUM, ERBIUM, THULIUM, YTERRBIUM.

5F SERIES:-

The space between RADIUM and LAWRENSIUM is indicated with 2 stars that indicates 5F SERIES. ACTINUM, THORIUM, POTACTINUM, URANIUM, NEPTUNIUM, PLUTONIUM, AMERCIUM, CURIUM, BERKELIUM, CALIFORNIUM, EINSTINIUM, FERMIUM, MENDALIVIUM, NOBLEIUM.
